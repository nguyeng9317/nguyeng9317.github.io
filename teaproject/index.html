Purpose

The purpose of this home page is to talk about tea. Tea is a drink that is sacred in a vast amount of cultures. It is famous for its many health benefits, and ability to sooth ones nerves. There are a variety of flavors and categories of tea, each with their own unique purpose.

Today tea is made by putting a bag of dried leaves and herbs into water. The water is usually hot making it easy for the flavor to expand into the drink. This is because the pores in the leaves open up and let all the flavor out.
