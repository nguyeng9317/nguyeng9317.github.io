Classifiaction of Tea

Tea is classified into different groups based on what color and leaves they use. Tea also has different sides, for example, herbal tea is often paired with honey or a squeeze of lemon. Whereas, milk is usually added to black tea.

Tea leaves come from different parts of the world and can be combined to make a specific flavor. Tea is also grouped into specific categories. Herbal tea is more fruit or nature tasting. Some examples of this is coconut ginger, lavender, or chamomile tea. And black tea is much darker and usually paired with milk, such as earl grey tea.
